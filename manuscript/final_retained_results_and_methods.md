# Final Retained Results and Methods

## Retained Main Claim

SwarmLineage-OT converts a native moscot OT pseudo-lineage into a finite-agent virtual-cell simulator. The retained branch-nucleation result is a computational order-parameter signature, interpreted as transient condensation-before-divergence, supported internally under the native teacher and by E1 external time-series analysis.

## Primary Model

M5_ot_swarm remains the primary mechanistic model. It is preferred over fuller variants because the retained claim depends on teacher fidelity and branch-order-parameter evidence, not architectural completeness. Unsupported modules are excluded from the main claim.

## v1.2 Findings

- Topological neighbour rules can reproduce the condensation-before-divergence signature in the internal and E1 settings.
- Metric-radius rules can also reproduce the signature, and random-neighbour controls are not clean in every dataset.
- Therefore the v1.2 local-rule conclusion is dataset-specific rather than a standalone topological-neighbour mechanism.
- The best fitted topological k is 2. A 5-9 neighbour rule is not uniquely supported.
- Susceptibility analysis is weak and should be framed as a high-susceptibility branch-window diagnostic only.
- The maximum-entropy minimal model is acceptable as a prototype diagnostic, not as proof of the mechanism.
- Local perturbation propagation is localized in silico and remains exploratory.

## Retained Limitations

L2 clone-aware validation fails under the current operationalization, so clone-level support is not established. Diffusion remains an encoded control-law recovery. Birth/death, memory and CCI remain unsupported. No experimental confirmation or causal conclusion is claimed.
