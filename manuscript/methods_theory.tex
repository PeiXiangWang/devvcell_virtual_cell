\section{SwarmLineage-OT finite-agent dynamics}
Each virtual cell agent \(i\) has latent state \(z_i(t)\), fate belief \(q_i(t)\), age or cell-cycle state \(a_i(t)\), local density/resource score \(r_i(t)\), optional spatial coordinate \(s_i(t)\), and memory exposure \(m_i(t)\). We model the latent dynamics as
\[
dz_i =
\left[
v_{\theta}^{\mathrm{intrinsic}}(z_i,t)
+ v^{\mathrm{OT}}(z_i,t)
+ v_{\theta}^{\mathrm{swarm}}(z_i,\mathcal{N}_i,r_i,q_i)
+ v_{\theta}^{\mathrm{CCI}}(z_i,\mathcal{C}_i)
- \nabla U_{\mathrm{manifold}}(z_i)
\right]dt
+ \sigma_{\theta}(z_i,t,H_i,r_i,a_i)dW_i .
\]
The birth and death hazards are non-negative softplus functions of lineage pressure, local density, cell-cycle score, OT growth proxy and fate uncertainty. In the mean-field limit, the empirical cell distribution satisfies a continuity-diffusion-reaction equation with birth and death source terms. The OT teacher supplies pseudo-lineage supervision through temporal couplings, barycentric descendant targets, fate probabilities, transition entropy and mass expansion estimates.
